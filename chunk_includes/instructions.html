In this experiment, you will be doing two tasks at the same time. First, at the start of each trial, you will see grids like this one:

After a short period of time, these grids disappear. At the end of each trial, you are presented with an empty grid and you must recreate the pattern that you saw at the start of the trial. It is important that you recreate the grid as accurately as possible.

In between, you will see a picture with a sentence describing the picture. It is your task to indicate if the sentence is a good description of the picture.

Press ‘1’ if you consider the sentence a good description of the picture; otherwise press ‘2’.

You will now be given two practice trials to familiarize yourself with the experiment. We are interested in your spontaneous responses, so don't think too long before answering. Good luck!
